My story

After graduating from a well known finance school I found myself drawn more towards coding and web development than to finance.

I've dabbled in a lot of different languages and techniques including HTML, CSS, some Javascript and C#. I've tried React and Angular and Node.js. I also have an account on Github and Stackoverflow.

Websites that have been my major source of knowledge are Udemy, FreeCodeCamp and Youtube of course. In addition to full-time studies, I am a full-time mother of four wild, crazy and wonderful boys.
